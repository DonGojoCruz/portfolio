My Journey

With over 3 years of experience as a Frontend Developer, I've shaped digital products for both startups and established companies. My career began in web design, where I developed a strong eye for aesthetics and user-centered interfaces. As I transitioned into frontend development, I focused on creating seamless experiences that drive engagement and business growth.

I've worked across industries like FinTech, eCommerce, and SaaS, gaining a deep understanding of building for different user needs while ensuring business goals are met. From MVPs to fully operational platforms, I take a clean-code and user-centered approach to every project.

I've built design systems, developed scalable component libraries, and collaborated with cross-functional teams to bring ideas from concept to reality.

In recent years, I've helped startups achieve milestones — from building MVPs to launching products that reached thousands of users. I'm passionate about creating impactful digital experiences that drive real success.
